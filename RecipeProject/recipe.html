Tasty Momo Recipe

Ingredients

Here are the essential Ingredients for making Delicious Momo.....

  • Dough 2 Cups all purpose Flour
  • Water (as needed)
  • 1/2 teaspoon salt
  • filling : 500g Chicken or Cabbage
  • 1 small Onion, finely chopped
  • 2 gralic cloves , Minced
  • 1 tablespoon ginger paste
  • 1 green chilly (finely chopped)
  • salt and pepper and timur to taste
  • 1 tablespoon soya sauce

Direction

  1. Prepare the Dough ; In a boul ,mix the flour with salt gradually add water and kneed until you form a smooth dough , cover and let it rest for 330 minutes.
  2. Prepare the Fillings ; In another boul Combine the Chicken or Cabbage , Chopped online, garlic, ginger, green chilly.season with salt pepper and soya sauce according to your taste.
  3. Divide the dough into small balls.roll each ball into a thin circular disc.
  4. Place a spoonful of filling in the center of each disc.
  5. Fold the edges and pleat to seat the momo properly.
  6. Steam the momos in a steamer for about 15-20 minutes until the dougb is cooked and filling in tender.
